Party Hall Guidelines

1. Booking & Usage Rules

  • Residents can book the Party Hall based on availability through the Estate Management Office.
  • The hall must be booked in advance by submitting the request form and paying the applicable charges and caution deposit.
  • Each Party Hall is equipped with 50 chairs.
  • If only one party is scheduled during a slot, an additional 50 chairs can be provided on request.
  • Booking slots are limited to a maximum of 8 hours.
  • Party Hall usage beyond 4 hours will attract additional charges for the next 4-hour block.
  • Applicable Charges: ₹3,500 for 4 hours, ₹7,000 for 8 hours.
  • Water supply pressure may be affected if the guest count exceeds 100 due to overhead tank refill delays.

2. Decoration & Cooking Restrictions

  • Cooking is not permitted inside the Party Hall or clubhouse kitchen.
  • LPG cylinders, tandoors, or open flame cooking equipment are strictly prohibited for safety reasons.
  • Use of electric fryers or induction stoves is also not allowed inside the Party Hall.
  • Only heating/serving of pre-cooked food is allowed, without any live cooking setups.
  • Decoration Rules:
    • No drilling, hammering nails, or using double-sided tape on walls, ceilings, or windows.
    • Residents must use the existing hooks and nails provided for decoration.
    • Stage and wall decorations must be completed in a safe and non-damaging manner.
    • Decorations are allowed only 2 hours before the event starts.

3. Waste Management

  • All event waste must be collected in proper garbage disposal bags.
  • Housekeeping staff will collect the waste at the end of the event if it is packed and ready.
  • Residents must ensure all garbage is removed from the premises before vacating the hall.
  • Any leftover food, decoration material, or party items must be cleared without causing damage or stains to floors and furniture.
  • Improper garbage disposal or spillage will lead to deductions from the security deposit.

4. Parking & Delivery Rules

  • Guest Parking: There is no designated guest parking space at the clubhouse.
  • Guests are advised to be dropped off at the Clubhouse and park their vehicles outside Gate A or Gate B.
  • Delivery & Setup Vehicles:
    • Vendors and goods vehicles must park outside the main gates.
    • Loading and unloading is permitted near the clubhouse for a maximum of 20 minutes only.
  • The clubhouse lift cannot be used for moving heavy items. Large materials must be carried via stairs.

5. Damage & Security Deposit

  • Residents must submit a refundable caution deposit:
    • ₹5,000 for up to 4 hours
    • ₹10,000 for up to 8 hours
    • To be submitted via cheque before the event date
  • This cheque will be returned after the event, subject to:
    • No damage to furniture, fixtures, or hall structure
    • No unauthorized activity or rule violations
    • Proper garbage disposal and cleaning compliance
  • Any damage to Party Hall assets or Clubhouse property will be assessed, and charges will be deducted from the deposit.
  • If the damage exceeds the deposit, the resident will be billed for the difference.
