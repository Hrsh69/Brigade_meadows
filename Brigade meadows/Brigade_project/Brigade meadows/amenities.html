Amenities

Explore the world-class facilities available to residents.

Swimming Pool

  • Separate ladies' batch (3:30 PM – 5:50 PM, except Mondays)
  • Cleaned regularly as per schedule
  • Proper attire required (no casuals)
  • No food, alcohol, or pets allowed

Gym

  • Open 5 AM – 12 PM & 4 PM – 10 PM
  • Entry with valid amenities ID only
  • Proper gym shoes mandatory
  • No food or outside footwear allowed

Party Hall

  • Bookable in 4-hour slots with security deposit
  • No cooking/tandoors allowed inside
  • Strict decoration rules to avoid wall damage
  • Extra charges for time extension

Children's Play Area

  • For children under 12 only
  • No pets, smoking, or sand throwing allowed
  • Children must be supervised by adults

Pushpak Viman (Buggy)

  • Only for senior citizens & differently abled
  • Ticket-based access, managed by forum
  • Only small bags allowed; no trolleys

Pet Policy

  • Pets allowed with registration & vaccinations
  • Leash and poop scooper mandatory in public
  • No feeding in corridors or common areas

Waste Management

  • Segregation: Wet, Dry, Reject & E-Waste
  • Collection daily at 8:30 AM
  • E-Bin available near basement lift

Security & Surveillance

  • 24/7 patrol + CCTV monitoring
  • MyGate for visitor & delivery control
  • Emergency readiness protocols in place

About Us

ABOUT BRIGADE MEADOWS

Brigade Meadows is not just a residential community — it's a lifestyle destination that blends greenery, modern infrastructure, and community-focused living. With thoughtfully designed amenities and a commitment to sustainability, we aim to provide an environment that supports healthy, secure, and fulfilling urban life for all residents.

Preserving our most vital resource

Water Conservation

At our core, we are dedicated to pioneering sustainable water management practices. We believe in redefining how water is used, treated, and conserved, setting new benchmarks for environmental responsibility and addressing the critical challenges of water scarcity globally.

Our commitment extends to implementing advanced wastewater treatment systems and promoting water-efficient technologies across all our operations. We strive to educate and empower communities and stakeholders to embrace water-saving habits, fostering a collective endeavor towards a greener future.
