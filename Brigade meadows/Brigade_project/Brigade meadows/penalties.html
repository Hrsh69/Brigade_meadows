Rule Violations & Penalties

Maintaining harmony and safety in Brigade Meadows through clear, fair penalties.

Low Severity – ₹500 Fine
  • Smoking in public areas (1st time)
  • Walking pets without leash or poop scooper
  • Honking after 10 PM
  • Loud noise/music disturbances
  • Throwing garbage in public areas
  • Keeping items/footwear in lobbies
Medium Severity – ₹1000 Fine
  • Repeat of low violations
  • Using altered silencers (1st or 2nd time)
  • Alcohol consumption in public areas
  • Driving through no-entry zones
  • Violation of facility rules (pool, gym, etc.)
  • Move-in/out after 6 PM or on holidays
Critical Violations – ₹5000+ & Legal Action
  • Manhandling / physical assault
  • Sexual harassment or abuse of minors
  • Major property damage
  • Repeat of medium violations
  • Any act endangering safety of residents
