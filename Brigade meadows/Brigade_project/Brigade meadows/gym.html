Gym Usage Guidelines

Welcome to our gym! To ensure a safe, respectful, and effective workout environment for everyone, please carefully read and adhere to the following guidelines. Your cooperation helps us maintain a top-notch facility.

Operating Hours

  • Morning: 5:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Access & Entry

  1. Access: Entry is allowed only with a valid Amenities ID card.
  2. Logbook: Members must record their entry and exit times in the logbook.
  3. Age Restriction: The gym is open to individuals 16 years and above. Those under 18 require parental consent.

Personal Conduct & Hygiene

  1. Footwear: Only proper gym shoes are allowed. No sandals, boots, slippers, open-toed shoes, or bare feet.
  2. Outside Footwear: Strictly prohibited inside the gym.
  3. Hygiene: Wipe down machines and equipment after use.
  4. Respect Others:
    • Maintain personal space.
    • Use headphones for music.
    • Keep phone conversations quiet.
  5. Food & Beverages: No food is allowed inside the gym.
  6. Ventilation & AC: Members are requested to keep windows and doors closed and use the air conditioning.

Equipment Usage & Care

  1. Cardio Equipment: Usage is limited to 15 minutes during peak hours.
  2. Weights Handling: Do not drop, slam, or throw weights on the floor.
  3. Equipment Usage: Return weights and equipment to their designated places after use.
  4. Damaged Equipment: Report any malfunctioning or damaged equipment to management and do not use it.
  5. Equipment Care: Damaging gym equipment will result in disciplinary action and fines.

Important Notices

  • Outsiders are strictly prohibited from using the gym. Violators will face strict action.
  • Management is not responsible for any injuries sustained while using gym facilities.
  • Management is not responsible for any lost or stolen personal belongings.
  • Any act of theft will result in blacklisting and strict action by management.
  • Timings will be reviewed periodically by the Estate Management on the advice of the Executive Committee.

Thank you for being a responsible and considerate gym member. If you have any questions or require assistance, please do not hesitate to speak with the gym staff or management.
