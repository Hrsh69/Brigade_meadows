Security SOP

Standard Operating Procedures for a safe and secure community.

General Guidelines

  • Security staff must wear uniforms and ID cards at all times.
  • Professional and polite behavior is mandatory.
  • All suspicious activities must be reported to Estate Management or EC.
  • Personnel must be trained in fire safety, first aid, and use of walkie-talkies.

Access Control & Visitor Management

  • Visitor entries must be logged via MyGate or physical register.
  • Delivery entry only upon resident approval.
  • Service providers require ID and prior approval.
  • No entry for unregistered salespersons or marketers.

Vehicle & Parking Management

  • Only authorized vehicles permitted; visitors must register.
  • Enforce parking rules and maintain emergency vehicle access.
  • Conduct patrols to identify violations.

Gate Security & Patrol Duties

  • Guards must be present at designated gates and patrol regularly.
  • Ensure all common area gates are locked after operational hours.
  • Monitor CCTV and report unusual activity.

Emergency Response

  • Respond to emergencies like theft, fire, or medical issues immediately.
  • Inform emergency services and EC as needed.
  • Keep firefighting equipment and evacuation protocols ready.

Handling Deliveries & Staff

  • No items should be received by security on behalf of residents.
  • Domestic staff must be logged at every entry and exit.

CCTV Monitoring

  • Continuous surveillance is required; tampering must be reported.
  • Footage access is limited to authorized personnel.

Incident Reporting

  • Maintain daily reports of incidents, patrols, and visitor logs.
  • Report thefts or security breaches immediately to EC.
  • Submit registers to Estate Office every 2 months.

Code of Conduct

  • No use of phones, smoking, drinking, or leaving post during duty hours.
  • Violations may lead to fines or termination.

Supervisor Responsibilities

  • Ensure guard compliance with SOP.
  • Conduct random checks and training sessions.
  • Coordinate with EC for improvements and emergency drills.

Review & Compliance

  • SOP is reviewed periodically.
  • Updates must be communicated to all personnel.
  • Non-compliance may result in corrective action or termination.

FREQUENTLY ASK QUESTION

Do you have questions?

What types of properties are available at Brigade Meadows? +

Brigade Meadows offers a diverse range of residential properties including spacious apartments, luxurious villas, and modern townhouses, designed to cater to various family sizes and preferences. Each property type is crafted with high-quality materials and contemporary designs.

What amenities are included for residents? +

Residents at Brigade Meadows have access to a comprehensive suite of amenities. These include a state-of-the-art fitness center, a sparkling swimming pool, landscaped gardens, children's play areas, a multi-purpose hall, 24/7 security, and dedicated parking spaces. Additional facilities like a clubhouse and sports courts are also available.

Is Brigade Meadows a pet-friendly community? +

Yes, Brigade Meadows is a pet-friendly community. We understand that pets are part of your family, and we have designated areas and policies in place to ensure a harmonious living environment for both pet owners and non-pet owners. Please refer to our detailed pet policy for more information.

What are the payment plan options for purchasing a property? +

We offer flexible payment plans to make your home ownership dream a reality. Our sales team can provide detailed information on various options, including installment plans, construction-linked payments, and partnerships with leading banks for home loan assistance. We recommend scheduling a consultation to discuss the best option for you.

How is the security managed within Brigade Meadows? +

Security is a top priority at Brigade Meadows. We employ a multi-layered security system that includes 24/7 manned security personnel, CCTV surveillance across common areas, controlled access points, and intercom facilities in each residence. We strive to provide a safe and secure environment for all our residents.
