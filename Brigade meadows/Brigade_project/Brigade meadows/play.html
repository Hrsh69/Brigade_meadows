Children's Play Area – Usage Guidelines

Welcome to our Children's Play Area! To ensure a safe, clean, and fun environment for all our young visitors, please observe the following rules:

  1. Age Restriction: The play area is strictly for **children under the age of 12**.
  2. No Throwing: Throwing sand on play equipment or footpaths is **prohibited**. Please keep sand in the designated areas.
  3. No Pets: For the safety and hygiene of all children, **pets are not allowed** in the play area.
  4. No Littering: **Littering is strictly prohibited**. Please help us keep the area clean by using the designated bins for all trash.
  5. Proper Equipment Use: Play equipment must be used **appropriately and as intended**. Misuse can lead to damage and injuries.
  6. Safe Play: **Running, pushing, or shoving is not allowed** to prevent accidents and ensure a safe play environment for everyone.
  7. No Smoking: **Smoking is strictly prohibited** within the entire play area.
  8. Adult Supervision: Children must be **supervised by a responsible adult at all times**. Adults are accountable for the behavior and safety of the children in their care.
  9. Toddler Swing Use: The toddler swing is specifically reserved for **children up to 4 years of age**.

Important Notes:

  • **Strict action will be taken against unauthorized individuals** using the amenities.
  • The management is **not responsible for any injuries sustained** while using these facilities. Please ensure children play responsibly and adults supervise diligently.

Thank you for helping us maintain a wonderful and safe play area for our community's children!
